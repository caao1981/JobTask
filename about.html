About Us

Tabout-us-left About JobTask JobTask UK LTD - trading as JobTask, established in January 2016 as a Sole Proprietorship and later transitioning to a Private Limited Company in January 2022, is a global leader in the on-demand service marketplace. Our mission is simple: to bring essential services to your fingertips with just a few clicks. Our Journey Our journey began when our Founder, Kundayi Matambanadzo, operated as a Sole Trader. During this time, he personally experienced and listened to countless customer complaints about other service providers – challenges that won't be detailed here for obvious reasons. Recognizing an industry gap and the need for a paradigm shift in service standards, JobTask was born. Our Commitment to You At JobTask, we place customer needs above all else. Our commitment is evident from the moment you book a service with us until its seamless completion or delivery. We understand that excellence is not just a goal; it's an unwavering commitment. Why Choose JobTask Unparalleled Convenience: We believe in making your life easier. Our platform allows you to access a wide range of services with unparalleled convenience. Whether you need home services like cleaning and furniture assembly or on-demand professionals such as mobile barbers, mobile hairdressers, or mobile nail technicians and many more, JobTask is your go-to choice. Quality Assurance: Your trust matters to us. We meticulously screen and select every service provider on our platform to ensure they meet our high standards. Rest assured, you're in capable hands. Customer-Centric Approach: At JobTask, we're all about you, the customer. Our user-centric philosophy guides everything we do, from our user-friendly platform to our responsive customer support. Transparency and Trust: We believe in transparency. Detailed service provider profiles, customer reviews, and upfront pricing ensure you have all the information you need to make informed choices. Our Dedicated Team's Commitment Our dedicated team is committed to working hard to achieve our goal of becoming the industry leader. We don't take this responsibility lightly. Join us on this journey Join us on this journey of convenience, reliability, and excellence. Experience the JobTask difference today.
